About Me

I am a very active and energetic person. I love doing sports, as it makes me feel good both physically and mentally. For me, it's very important to stay healthy and fit. Additionally, I always strive to grow as a person and improve in different aspects of my life. I like to dedicate time to reflection and self-knowledge in order to achieve my goals and objectives.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games and reading. These activities allow me to disconnect from the routine and enjoy a moment of leisure. I am a curious person and I am always willing to experiment with new things, whether they are games or books that make me learn and discover interesting things.

Lastly, I am passionate about programming and everything related to technology. I consider it to be a valuable skill in today's world and I am working hard to improve my technical abilities. I like to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and advances in this field and I am always willing to learn new things.
